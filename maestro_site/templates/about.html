What is Maestro?

Maestro turns all your mobile devices into a speaker system, letting you control which instruments each speaker plays.

Who Made Maestro?

Maestro is Oberlin College students:

  • Max Mellman, Founder & Designer
  • Luke Lovett, Lead Developer
  • Richard Townsend, Assistant Developer
  • Joshua Selesnick, Programming Consultant

You can follow us on Twitter or like us on Facebook.

Maestro is Currently in Alpha

At this time, we cannot guarantee the success of your listening sessions. You may find a higher success rate if you use two laptops, and are connected to the same network, but you are welcome to test other devices in larger quantities as well. We will continue optimizing our synchronization technology. Have ideas? Get in touch!
